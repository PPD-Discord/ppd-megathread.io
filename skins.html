What are 'Mods,' 'Menu Skins,' and 'Skins?'

When we say 'mods,' we are referring to modifications that make the game look better/more like an official Project Diva game during gameplay (when you're playing a song), or mods that change your rhythm gaming experience in general.

When we say 'menu skins,' we are referring to modifications that make the game's menus look different (so, anything before/after playing a song.)

We recommend you get both mods and a menu skin before starting to play PPD!

NOTE REGARDING MENU SKINS: You can only have one menu skin at a time, so choose wisely!

You can have as many mods as you want, but they won't all work together at the same time. (The REAL PDA-FT Skin X 4.0 mod is an example of one of these - it looks gorgeous, but can only be enabled on its own and with/without CSInput).

Skins are .mod files, so they are also placed in the 'mods' folder in your 'PPD' folder and activated the same way mods are activated (open menu with square->mods->checkmark).
