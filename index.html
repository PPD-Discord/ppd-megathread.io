Welcome!

You have reached the community megathread for PPDxxx.

PPDxxx, AKA "Project Project Diva," is a fanmade project made by a Japanese developer called KHCmaster.

With PPD, players can create and play their own and others' fanmade Project Diva charts!

Playing with others in multiplayer mode is also possible.

Official PPD Website

This megathread is NOT an official PPD website; it is entirely fanmade.

It is designed to help players new to PPD.

To download charts and make a PPD account, visit the official PPD website*.
